PearBrowser Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

PearBrowser is a peer-to-peer web browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. PearBrowser does not have analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking of any kind. There is no account system and no data is sent to our servers.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Bookmarks — sites you save for quick access
  • Browsing history — URLs you visit (can be cleared in Settings)
  • Settings — your preferences (catalog URL, private mode)
  • Published sites — websites you create with the built-in editor

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

Peer-to-Peer Networking

PearBrowser uses peer-to-peer (P2P) networking via the Hyperswarm protocol. When you browse P2P content:

  • Your IP address is visible to peers you connect with. This is inherent to how P2P networking works.
  • Content is fetched directly from peers or via a relay gateway — no centralized server stores or logs your browsing activity.
  • The Distributed Hash Table (DHT) is used for peer discovery. Your participation in the DHT is limited to finding peers for content you request.

Camera

PearBrowser requests camera access solely for scanning QR codes containing hyper:// addresses. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or processed beyond reading the QR code data.

Third-Party Services

PearBrowser does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app may connect to a relay gateway (configurable in Settings) to fetch P2P content over HTTPS as a fallback.

Children's Privacy

PearBrowser does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
